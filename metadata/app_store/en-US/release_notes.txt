Motion Preview has been redesigned as a focused macOS preview workspace.

- Preview SVGA, Lottie, images, SVG, animated images, and common video formats.
- Open each resource in its own window, sized to match the content when possible.
- Inspect format-specific dimensions, animation, video, and file details.
- Control playback, frame stepping, speed, looping, zoom, and canvas backgrounds when supported.
- Quickly reopen resources from the new Recent list.
- Use Motion Preview in English or Simplified Chinese based on the macOS language.
- Improved cold launch, document opening, window stability, and narrow-window layouts.
